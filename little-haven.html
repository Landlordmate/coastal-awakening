The Setting

You're in Oxwich Bay on the stunning Gower Peninsula — Britain's first Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty — just 25–30 minutes from Swansea. Close enough to reach easily. Wild enough to truly awaken.

Getting There

Swansea Airport 20–30 mins away. Cardiff Airport 1 hour 15 mins. Regular trains with short taxi rides available. Everything you need to arrive refreshed and stay focused.
