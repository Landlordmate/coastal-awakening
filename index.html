The Setting

Oxwich Bay,
Gower

Located on the Gower Peninsula, the UK's first Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty, this coastline offers space, stillness and perspective in equal measure. With golden sands, coastal paths, and breathtaking views, it's the perfect backdrop for rest and renewal.
